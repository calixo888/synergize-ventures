Dixon Landing Research Park, Fremont, CA

Dixon Landing Research Park consist of three commercial buildings (each on its own separate parcel), completed in 1985, totaling approximately 209,300 square feet. Each building is single story concrete construction, with interior clear heights of approximately 18 feet. The site totals approximately 13.7 acres and offers 574 parking stalls for a parking ratio of approximately 2.75 per 1,000 rentable square feet.
